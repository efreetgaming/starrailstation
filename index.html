Star Rail Station

Hop aboard the Astral Express and experience the galaxy's infinite wonders filled with adventure and thrills. Players will meet new companions across various worlds and maybe even run into some familiar faces. Overcome the struggles together caused by Stellaron and unravel the hidden truths behind it! May this journey lead us starward!


Gameplay

"Get ready for 'Honkai: Star Rail' – a thrilling, streamlined turn-based RPG with touch-based battles. Your squad wields basic Attacks, powerful Skills, and jaw-dropping Ultimate Attacks, all controlled with a tap. Speed dictates turn order, and managing shared Skill points is the key. Ready for action?"

Blaze Through Space

Discover 'Honkai: Star Rail,' a thrilling astral journey across three unique planets, from peaceful towns to challenging dungeons with puzzles. Solve puzzles for progression and bonus loot. The map helps you track chests. While not vast, the worlds are rich in detail, brimming with NPCs, side quests, and captivating lore.

Incredible Storyline

"Prepare for a visually striking and mature art style, reminiscent of 'Honkai Impact 3rd.' 'Honkai: Star Rail' boasts top-notch graphics and a killer soundtrack that amplifies the excitement. It's a sensory feast!"
