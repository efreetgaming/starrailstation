Gameplay

"Get ready for 'Honkai: Star Rail' – a thrilling, streamlined turn-based RPG with touch-based battles. Your squad wields basic Attacks, powerful Skills, and jaw-dropping Ultimate Attacks, all controlled with a tap. Speed dictates turn order, and managing shared Skill points is the key. Ready for action?"

Blaze Through Space

Discover 'Honkai: Star Rail,' a thrilling astral journey across three unique planets, from peaceful towns to challenging dungeons with puzzles. Solve puzzles for progression and bonus loot. The map helps you track chests. While not vast, the worlds are rich in detail, brimming with NPCs, side quests, and captivating lore.

Outstanding Animations

Explore Distinct Worlds — Discover the boundless universe filled with wonder 3, 2, 1, initiating warp! A space station with Curios sealed away, a foreign planet with an eternal winter, a starship hunting down abominations... Every stop of the Astral Express is a never-before-seen view of the galaxy! Explore fantastical worlds and civilizations, uncover mysteries beyond imagination, and set out on a journey of wonder!
